Contacting JavaSoft

 JDK 1.1 Contents

The Java Development Kit (JDK) is a product of Sun Microsystems, Inc. The JDK is developed by JavaSoft, a Sun Operating Company.

JavaSoft welcomes your comments and suggestions. You can contact us through email, or you can file a bug report. But please remember that JavaSoft does not offer free technical support. If you need help, try the JavaSoft Assistance Page, where you'll find links to FAQs, online tutorials, and many other resources.

Send Us Email

Feel free to send email directly to JavaSoft engineering teams. The JavaSoft Web Site has an address list. If you're not sure which team to contact, browse the Guide to New Features and look for a "Comments" link at the bottom of each page.

Send general comments to jdk-comments@java.sun.com.

File a Bug Report

You can use our bug tracking system to report bugs or request new features. Just go to our Bug Report Form.

Download/Installation Problems?

If you're having problems downloading or installing the JDK, tell us about them on our Web Site Feedback Form.
